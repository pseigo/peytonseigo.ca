It’s not you, it’s Internet Explorer...

Hi, thank you for visiting my site! I noticed you’re using Internet Explorer. Unfortunately, my layout doesn’t adapt well to your browser, but I’d love for you to stick around. Two modern browsers you could try are Mozilla Firefox or Google Chrome. Don’t worry, it’s quick and easy to upgrade!

Why not use Internet Explorer?

Microsoft advises against using Internet Explorer as your default browser.

Internet Explorer is a compatibility solution. We’re not supporting new web standards for it and, while many sites work fine, developers by and large just aren’t testing for Internet Explorer these days.

I’ll still be here when you get your shiny new browser installed!
